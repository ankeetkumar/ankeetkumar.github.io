🍗 Chicken Dum Biryani — Ankeet's Recipe
Ingredients
  • 750g bone-in chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2.5 cups aged basmati rice (soaked 30 min)
  • 1 cup thick yogurt · Fried onions
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chilli + 1 tsp biryani masala
  • Whole spices: bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, star anise
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk
  • Fresh mint & coriander · Ghee · Rose water
Method
  1. Marinate: Mix chicken with yogurt, spices, half fried onions, mint & ginger-garlic paste. Rest 2+ hours.
  2. Parboil rice: Cook in salted water with whole spices until 70% done. Drain.
  3. Layer: Chicken on bottom, rice layer, saffron milk, fried onions, mint, ghee, rose water.
  4. Dum: Seal pot with dough or foil. High heat 5 min, then lowest flame 25–30 min.
  5. Rest: Let sit 10 min. Serve with raita.
💡 The secret is patience — never rush the dum. The aroma will tell you when it's ready.
🫘 Rajma Chawal — The Doda Way
Ingredients
  • 1.5 cups red kidney beans (soaked overnight)
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed · 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1.5 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin · 1 tsp coriander · ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri red chilli · ½ tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil (the Dogri secret) · Salt
Method
  1. Pressure cook: Boil soaked rajma with salt until very soft (4–5 whistles).
  2. Masala: In mustard oil, splutter cumin. Add onions till golden. Add ginger-garlic, then tomato. Cook till oil separates.
  3. Spice: Add dry spices, stir 2 min.
  4. Combine: Add boiled rajma + water. Simmer 20 min until thickened.
  5. Finish: Garam masala + coriander. Serve over steamed rice.
💡 Mustard oil is non-negotiable — it gives it that Dogri soul. My mother's recipe, passed on from her mother.

My Wakanda

A Heaven on Earth

Like the fictional nation of Wakanda — abundant, extraordinary, and known only to those who belong to it — Doda holds a secret grandeur the rest of the world has yet to fully discover. Its landscapes are mythic: forested ridges that vanish into clouds, waterfalls thundering through limestone gorges, and village paths winding past walnut orchards and ancient shrines.

The people of Doda are as resilient and warm as the land itself. With a confluence of Dogri, Kashmiri, Pahari, and Gojri cultures, the region carries a richness of language, music, and cuisine entirely its own. Growing up speaking Bhaderwahi and Sarazi — dialects unique to the Chenab belt — I carry Doda in every syllable I utter.

Science has taken me far, from Doda to Bangalore to international conferences in Europe. But my compass always points home. When the papers are written and the data shared with the world, I intend to spend my quieter years exactly where it all began: sitting on a hillside in Doda, watching the mist roll over the mountains.

Doda is my Wakanda. A beautiful, hidden heaven on earth, and it is where I belong.

Scientific Work

Research & Expertise

Focus Areas

What I Investigate

🧬

Viral Genomics & Evolution

Characterising clade-defining SNPs in rabies virus (RABV) to improve global surveillance and understand its evolutionary dynamics to improve surveillance and decode molecular changes underlying host-switching events.

🌐

Phylodynamics

Bayesian phylogenetics using BEAST to trace viral spread, emergence events, and spatio-temporal evolutionary patterns across continents.

🔬

Proteomics & Host Response

Profiling the proteome of rabid canine brain tissue to uncover dysregulated immune signalling and neuronal pathways during viral pathogenesis.

🦠

One-Health & Zoonoses

Integrating genomic, epidemiological, and ecological data to understand spillover dynamics in neglected pathogens including mpox virus and LSDV.
